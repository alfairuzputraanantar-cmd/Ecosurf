Getting Started
How to get started with CocaCoy ERP?
Follow these steps to get started:
1
Open the Register page and create a new account with your email and password.
2
Login using your newly created account.
3
Go to Settings → Store Profile and complete your business information.
4
Add your first product on the Products page.
5
The dashboard will automatically display real-time statistics of your data.
What is displayed on the Dashboard page?
The Dashboard displays a real-time inventory summary:
  • Total Products — number of distinct product types registered
  • Inventory Value — total value of stock (price × quantity)
  • Low Stock Items — products with running low stock
  • Total Stock Units — sum of all individual items in stock
  • Stock by Category — bar chart grouping products by category
  • Recently Added — last 5 products added to the system
  • Low Stock Alerts — quick list of products needing restock
How to toggle dark/light mode?
Click the 🌙 / ☀️ icon in the top right corner of any page to toggle between dark and light modes. Your preference is automatically saved in your browser. You can also configure this by going to Settings → Appearance → Dark / Light Mode.
Product Management
How do I add a new product?
1
Navigate to the Products page from the sidebar.
2
Click the + Add Product button in the top right.
3
Fill in the form: Product Name, Category, Price, and Initial Stock Quantity.
4
Click Save Product — the data is instantly saved to Firebase and appears in your list.

💡 Available categories: Food, Drink, Electronics, Clothing, Cosmetics, Goods, Others.

How do I edit or update product stock?
To edit a product, click the ✏️ Edit icon on the corresponding row in the products table. The edit modal will appear with pre-filled details — change any necessary values and click Save Changes. All modifications are synchronized to Firebase immediately and recorded in the History.
Can a deleted product be recovered?
No. Deleting a product is permanent in the Firebase database. However, a deletion log is still recorded on the History page (labeled as Deleted) so you can review what products existed previously with their latest details.

💡 Tip: It is recommended to reduce stock to 0 instead of deleting the item, keeping historical data intact.
How do low stock notifications work?
The system automatically monitors stock for every product. When stock is ≤ the threshold limit (default: 5 units), the 🔔 icon in the topbar displays a red badge with the number of low-stock products.
  • Red (Critical) — stock = 0, restock immediately!
  • Yellow (Warning) — stock is running low
You can change the default threshold by going to Settings → Inventory → Default Low Stock Threshold.
How do I search and filter products?
On the Products page you can use:
  • Search bar — type the product name to filter results in real-time
  • Category Filter — not available natively here, but you can search by category name
  • Column Sort — click on table headers to sort items (if implemented)
All active filters work together simultaneously without page reload.
Reports & Charts
What can I see on the Charts page?
The Charts page displays visual analytics of your inventory:
  • Bar Chart — top products by stock quantity
  • Pie/Donut Chart — product distribution by category
  • Line Chart — item additions trend over the last 14 days
Data is fetched in real-time from Firebase, ensuring the graphs constantly reflect up-to-date conditions.
What is the History page and how do I read it?
The History page logs all changes made to your products:
  • 🟢 Added — new products registered
  • 🔵 Edited — product details updated (name/price/stock)
  • 🔴 Deleted — products removed from the database
Use the filter tabs at the top to narrow down activities by action type, or use the search box to find specific logs.
Can data be exported to Excel or PDF?
A native export feature is currently unavailable. However, you can press Ctrl+P to print any page layout to PDF. Proper CSV/Excel export functionality is on the roadmap for an upcoming update. Keep an eye on our Changelog!
Account & Security
What if I forget my password?
On the Login page, click the "Forgot Password?" link. Enter your registered email address and the system will send you a password reset link. Please verify your Spam/Junk folders if you can't find it in your inbox.

Alternatively: Contact your system admin via the support channels listed below.
How do I change my password?
Go to Settings → Account Security. Type your current password, enter your new password (minimum 8 characters), and confirm it. Click Change Password to save the update.
How do I log out safely?
Click the Sign Out button positioned at the bottom of the left sidebar. You will automatically be redirected to the Login page. Always remember to log out, especially on shared devices, to protect business data.
Where is my data stored? Is it secure?
All product and transaction data are housed securely on Google Firebase Firestore — an encrypted cloud database maintained globally by Google. Your data is:
  • ✅ Hosted securely on enterprise Google servers
  • ✅ Automatically backed up by Firebase
  • ✅ Accessible across any device with authentication
  • ✅ Synchronized in real-time without constant page refreshes
