Quick Start Guide
How to get started with CocaCoy ERP?
Follow this 5-step workflow:
1
Add Products — Go to Products page, click + Add Product. Fill in name, category, buy/sell price, stock, and unit. A barcode is generated automatically.
2
Manage Inventory — Edit stock anytime. Use Restock for incoming goods. Low-stock alerts appear automatically on the Dashboard.
3
Use the Cashier — Go to Cashier. Search products manually or scan their barcode. Add them to your cart.
4
Process Payment & Receipt — Choose Cash or QRIS. After checkout, a printable receipt (bon) is generated automatically.
5
Monitor Analytics — Your Dashboard and Charts page show live revenue, profit, best-sellers, and AI-powered recommendations.
What is displayed on the Dashboard?
The Dashboard shows a real-time business overview:
  • Total Products, Inventory Value, Low Stock — top stat cards
  • Revenue Today / This Week — with trend indicators vs. yesterday/last week
  • Est. Profit Today / This Month — calculated from buy/sell price difference
  • Total Transactions This Month
  • Stock by Category — bar chart
  • Recently Added Products — last 5 items
  • Low Stock Alerts — products needing restock
  • 🧠 CocaCoy AI Insights — smart business recommendations powered by Gemini AI
How do I toggle dark/light mode?
Click the 🌙 / ☀️ icon in the top-right corner on any page. Your preference is automatically saved in your browser.
Cashier & POS
How do I process a sale transaction?
1
Open the Cashier page from the sidebar.
2
Search for products manually OR click Scan Barcode to scan using your device's camera.
3
Click a product to add it to the cart. Adjust quantity as needed.
4
Open the cart panel, choose Cash or QRIS, then click Process Transaction.
5
Stock is automatically deducted. A printable receipt (bon) will appear immediately.
How does barcode scanning work?
Every product in CocaCoy has an auto-generated barcode. On the Cashier page, click the Scan Barcode button. Allow camera access when prompted, then point your camera at the product barcode. The system will instantly find and add the product to your cart — no manual typing needed.

💡 This works on both desktop (with a webcam) and mobile (using the rear camera).
What is QRIS simulation?
When selecting QRIS as the payment method, a simulated QR code is displayed. In a real store scenario, customers would scan this QR using their banking/e-wallet app. In CocaCoy, this is a simulation mode — you confirm the payment manually by clicking Confirm Payment. The transaction is then processed exactly like a cash payment.
Can I print receipts after checkout?
Yes! After every successful transaction, a Transaction Receipt modal automatically appears. The receipt includes:
  • Your store name (from your profile)
  • Transaction date, time, and unique ID (e.g., CCY-TRX-20260510-XXXX)
  • Itemized list of products, quantities, and subtotals
  • Payment method and total amount
Click Print Receipt to open the browser print dialog. The receipt is optimized for thermal printers (80mm width) and prints in clean white/black — no dark mode UI.
AI Business Insights
What is CocaCoy AI Insights?
The 🧠 CocaCoy AI Insights card on the Dashboard is an AI-powered business assistant. It automatically analyzes your inventory and sales data and generates practical, actionable recommendations such as:
  • "Indomie sales are increasing. Consider restocking soon."
  • "Some products have high stock but no sales in 7 days."
  • "3 products are critically low on stock."
This feature is powered by Google Gemini AI. It works as a background business intelligence tool — not a chatbot.
How often do AI Insights update?
AI Insights are generated automatically when you load the Dashboard, and the result is saved (cached) for 20 minutes to avoid excessive API usage. This means:
  • Opening/refreshing the page within 20 minutes reuses the last insights
  • After 20 minutes, fresh insights are generated from your latest data
You can always get fresh insights immediately by clicking the Refresh button on the AI card, which bypasses the cache.
Product Management
How do I add a new product?
1
Navigate to the Products page from the sidebar.
2
Click the + Add Product button in the top right.
3
Fill in the form: Product Name, Category, Price, and Initial Stock Quantity.
4
Click Save Product — the data is instantly saved to Firebase and appears in your list.

💡 Available categories: Food, Drink, Electronics, Clothing, Cosmetics, Goods, Others.

How do I edit or update product stock?
To edit a product, click the ✏️ Edit icon on the corresponding row in the products table. The edit modal will appear with pre-filled details — change any necessary values and click Save Changes. All modifications are synchronized to Firebase immediately and recorded in the History.
Can a deleted product be recovered?
No. Deleting a product is permanent in the Firebase database. However, a deletion log is still recorded on the History page (labeled as Deleted) so you can review what products existed previously with their latest details.

💡 Tip: It is recommended to reduce stock to 0 instead of deleting the item, keeping historical data intact.
How do low stock notifications work?
The system automatically monitors stock for every product. When stock is ≤ the threshold limit (default: 5 units), the 🔔 icon in the topbar displays a red badge with the number of low-stock products.
  • Red (Critical) — stock = 0, restock immediately!
  • Yellow (Warning) — stock is running low
You can change the default threshold by going to Settings → Inventory → Default Low Stock Threshold.
How do I search and filter products?
On the Products page you can use:
  • Search bar — type the product name to filter results in real-time
  • Category Filter — not available natively here, but you can search by category name
  • Column Sort — click on table headers to sort items (if implemented)
All active filters work together simultaneously without page reload.
Reports & Charts
What can I see on the Charts page?
The Charts page displays visual analytics of your inventory:
  • Bar Chart — top products by stock quantity
  • Pie/Donut Chart — product distribution by category
  • Line Chart — item additions trend over the last 14 days
Data is fetched in real-time from Firebase, ensuring the graphs constantly reflect up-to-date conditions.
What is the History page and how do I read it?
The History page logs all changes made to your products:
  • 🟢 Added — new products registered
  • 🔵 Edited — product details updated (name/price/stock)
  • 🔴 Deleted — products removed from the database
Use the filter tabs at the top to narrow down activities by action type, or use the search box to find specific logs.
Can data be exported to Excel or PDF?
A native export feature is currently unavailable. However, you can press Ctrl+P to print any page layout to PDF. Proper CSV/Excel export functionality is on the roadmap for an upcoming update. Keep an eye on our Changelog!
Account & Security
What if I forget my password?
On the Login page, click the "Forgot Password?" link. Enter your registered email address and the system will send you a password reset link. Please verify your Spam/Junk folders if you can't find it in your inbox.

Alternatively: Contact your system admin via the support channels listed below.
How do I change my password?
Go to Settings → Account Security. Type your current password, enter your new password (minimum 8 characters), and confirm it. Click Change Password to save the update.
How do I log out safely?
Click the Sign Out button positioned at the bottom of the left sidebar. You will automatically be redirected to the Login page. Always remember to log out, especially on shared devices, to protect business data.
Where is my data stored? Is it secure?
All product and transaction data are housed securely on Google Firebase Firestore — an encrypted cloud database maintained globally by Google. Your data is:
  • ✅ Hosted securely on enterprise Google servers
  • ✅ Automatically backed up by Firebase
  • ✅ Accessible across any device with authentication
  • ✅ Synchronized in real-time without constant page refreshes
